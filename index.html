Bolt Online Learning Toolbox¶

Release:1.4
Date:June 11, 2010
Author:peter.prettenhofer@gmail.com

Bolt features discriminative learning of linear predictors (e.g. SVM or logistic regression) using stochastic gradient descent. Bolt is aimed at large-scale, high-dimensional and sparse machine-learning problems. In particular, problems encountered in information retrieval and natural language processing.
